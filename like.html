my name

My name is Youngji Sohn, written as 손 영지 in Hangeul. The first is my family name, and the latter is my given name. My late grandfather gave me this name.

It's pronounced /jʌŋ.dʑi/ in Korean, but "young-jee" is also a good approximation.
